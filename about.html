Our Story

Shakoai Foundation is more than just a home it’s a place of healing, learning, and growth. Founded with the belief that every child deserves love and opportunity, we provide shelter, education, and emotional support to children who have faced adversity. Through structured programs, mentorship, and a strong community network, we empower young lives to dream, achieve, and flourish.

Our Approach

Holistic Care: We nurture children’s physical, emotional, and intellectual well-being. Education & Skill Development: We equip children with knowledge and life skills for a brighter future. Community Involvement: We collaborate with individuals and organizations to maximize impact. Long-Term Support: We ensure children have the guidance they need as they transition into adulthood.

  • Volunteer Your Time: Join us in mentoring, tutoring, or simply offering companionship.
  • Donate: Support our mission by contributing to food, education, and wellness programs.
  • Sponsor a child: Make a lasting difference by funding a child's educational journey.
  • Spread Awareness: Share our mission and help us grow our community of supporters.
